Billionaires: Who are they and how did they get their wealth?

Everyone dreams of one day having money but who are the people alive today who currently have the most wealth, how did they get it and where do they come from? Can we learn anything about how someone like you or me should go about accumulating wealth or is it all a forgone conclusion?

(All figures are in USD)

OVERVIEW

Which Countries Have the Most Billionaires?

Clearly USA leads the way with over 700 billionaires and wealth seems to be concentrated around the USA, China, India and Germany. Interestingly, Africa has only 6 billionaires while Asia has over 1,000.

Which Industry has Made People the Most Money?

When looking at industries we can see technology has made over 2 trillion USD and leads the way comfortably. Finance and investments is a close second but the biggest surprise of all is fashion and retain; a whopping 1.6 trillion USD made.

DEMOGRAPHICS

Is there any Correlation Between Age and Wealth?

There does not seem to be any relation as we can see the top 50 most wealthy people are all grouped by wealth with their ages varying from the 30s to the late 90s. We can see from this however that you have no chance of accumulating wealth until you are at least 35 and it only seems to be more likely from there.

How Old are Billionaires?

Similarly to the scatterplot, the wealth is concentrated around the 50s to 70s with the largest proportion of billionaires coming in their 60s. This is what we would expect from a discipline that can only come from accumulating over long periods of time and when looking at who has inherited their money, it takes a long time to start from relative nothing and generate mass wealth.

BREAKDOWN

Are there Female Billionaires?

The current figures see only 97 females compared to 2,503 male billionaires. This is just 3.7%! However this is is also the highest number of female billionaires of all time.

Who has Daddy's Money?

Only a surprising 20% of billionaires have recieved their weatlh from their family with the majority coming from their own businesses and toil. Interestingly, the female make up is only 15% of those whom have inherited their wealth.
